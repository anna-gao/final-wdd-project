me

Hi! I'm Anna. I am a third-year student at UC Berkeley majoring in Data Science with an emphasis in Economics and minoring in Computer Science. Last summer, I was a Software Engineering intern at Hewlett Packard Enterprise in San Jose, CA on the Primera Storage team.

I am passionate about bridging the education gap in CS and increasing accessibility and diversity to the tech space. This semester, I served as Internal Vice President of ANova, an on-campus organization which seeks to provide computer science education and mentorship to students in under-resourced schools and marginalized communities.

In my free time, I enjoy playing volleyball, watching anime, and checking out new artists on Spotify.
